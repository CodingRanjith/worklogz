## Attendance Management

### Purpose
The Attendance Management module allows employees to check in and check out of work, track their attendance history, view weekly hours, and monitor daily earnings.

### Key Features

#### Check-In/Check-Out
- **Camera Verification**: Employees can capture a photo during check-in/check-out for verification
- **Location Tracking**: System automatically captures and verifies location
- **Work Mode Selection**: Choose between:
  - Office Mode: Working from office location
  - Remote Mode: Working from home or remote location
  - Hybrid Mode: Combination of office and remote work
- **Time Stamping**: Automatic recording of check-in and check-out times
- **Skip Camera Option**: Option to skip camera capture if needed

#### Attendance Cards
- **Today's Status**: View current day's attendance status
- **Check-In Time**: Display of check-in timestamp
- **Check-Out Time**: Display of check-out timestamp (if available)
- **Work Mode Indicator**: Shows selected work mode
- **Location Display**: Shows verified location

#### Weekly Hours Tracking
- **Current Week Summary**: View total hours worked in current week
- **Week Navigation**: Navigate between weeks to view historical data
- **Hours Breakdown**: Daily breakdown of working hours
- **Visual Representation**: Graphical display of weekly hours

#### Daily Earnings Card
- **Earnings Calculation**: Real-time calculation of daily earnings based on hours worked
- **Salary Rate**: Display of per-hour or per-day rate
- **Total Earnings**: Cumulative earnings display
- **Transaction History**: View of daily credit transactions

#### Activity Log
- **Attendance History**: Complete history of all check-in/check-out records
- **Date Filtering**: Filter attendance by specific dates
- **Activity Details**: View detailed information for each attendance entry
- **Export Capability**: Ability to view and track attendance patterns

#### Calendar View
- **Monthly Calendar**: Visual calendar showing attendance status for each day
- **Color Coding**: Different colors for:
  - Present days
  - Absent days
  - Leave days
  - Holidays
- **Date Selection**: Click on any date to view detailed attendance information
- **Holiday Indicators**: Company holidays marked on calendar

#### Holiday List
- **Company Holidays**: View all company holidays
- **Holiday Details**: Information about each holiday
- **Calendar Integration**: Holidays visible in attendance calendar

#### Timesheet Submission
- **Weekly Submission**: Submit timesheet entries weekly

- **Edit Capability**: Edit entries before submission

#### Timesheet History

- **Date Range Selection**: Select start and end dates
