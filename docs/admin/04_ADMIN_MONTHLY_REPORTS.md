# Admin Monthly Reports Documentation

## Overview
The Monthly Reports module provides comprehensive monthly summaries of all activities, performance metrics, and business data. It helps administrators review monthly performance and generate reports for stakeholders.

## Purpose
The Monthly Reports module enables administrators to:
- Generate comprehensive monthly summaries
- Review monthly performance metrics
- Track monthly trends
- Create reports for management
- Analyze monthly data

## Key Features

### Report Types

#### Attendance Reports
- **Monthly Attendance Summary**: Summary of attendance for the month
- **Attendance Statistics**: Detailed attendance statistics
- **Absence Report**: Report on absences
- **Late Arrival Report**: Report on late arrivals
- **Attendance Trends**: Monthly attendance trends

#### Employee Reports
- **Employee Activity Report**: Summary of employee activities
- **Employee Performance Report**: Monthly performance metrics
- **Employee Hours Report**: Total hours worked per employee
- **Employee Productivity Report**: Productivity metrics

#### Department Reports
- **Department Performance Report**: Performance by department
- **Department Attendance Report**: Attendance by department
- **Department Statistics**: Key statistics per department

#### Financial Reports
- **Salary Report**: Monthly salary summary
- **Payment Report**: Payment transactions for the month
- **Cost Report**: Operational costs
- **Revenue Report**: Revenue summary (if applicable)

#### Task Reports
- **Task Completion Report**: Tasks completed in the month
- **Task Status Report**: Status of all tasks
- **Task Performance Report**: Task performance metrics

#### Project Reports
- **Project Status Report**: Status of all projects
- **Project Progress Report**: Progress on projects
- **Project Timeline Report**: Project timelines

### Report Generation
- **Automatic Generation**: Reports generated automatically at month end
- **Manual Generation**: Generate reports on demand
- **Date Range Selection**: Select custom date ranges
- **Report Customization**: Customize report content

### Report Formats
- **PDF Export**: Export reports as PDF
- **Excel Export**: Export reports as Excel files
- **CSV Export**: Export data as CSV
- **Print**: Print reports directly

### Report Distribution
- **Email Distribution**: Send reports via email
- **Download**: Download reports
- **Share**: Share reports with stakeholders
- **Archive**: Archive reports for future reference

## Report Content

### Standard Sections
- **Executive Summary**: High-level overview
- **Key Metrics**: Important metrics and KPIs
- **Detailed Data**: Detailed breakdowns
- **Charts and Graphs**: Visual representations
- **Trends**: Trend analysis
- **Recommendations**: Actionable recommendations

## Usage
Administrators use Monthly Reports to:
1. Review monthly performance
2. Identify areas for improvement
3. Generate reports for management
4. Track monthly trends
5. Make strategic decisions

## Benefits
- **Comprehensive Overview**: Complete monthly overview
- **Performance Tracking**: Track performance over time
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Communicate with stakeholders
- **Historical Records**: Maintain historical records
- **Decision Support**: Support decision-making process
